Exchange, better!

TUMi is here to make your international experience better. No matter if you are an incoming student, doing your international mobility or a local student wanting to broaden your horizon.
We are a registered Association (Verein) with close connections to our friends in the TUM Global & Alumni Office. Our focus is the promotion of international spirit, tolerance in all facets of culture and the fostering of international understanding.
