Some more info about us and Munich

TUMi

We are a student association that has around 100 active members. TUMi was founded in 1995 and is part of ESN since 2008. In 2018, we had around 340 events with 19.000 participants. Every semester, we have 1.700 exchange students and around 10.000 international degree students that are potential participants of our activities. All our activities are open to German students to promote social interaction between international and local students.

MESA

The Munich Erasmus Student Association (MESA e.V.) is an association founded by former ERASMUS students studying at LMU Munich. Not only do we assist Erasmus students studying at LMU, but we also help integrate all international students of other local universities. We help students settle in Munich, answer their questions and try to make you‚Äôre their time in the Bavarian capital as pleasant as possible.
